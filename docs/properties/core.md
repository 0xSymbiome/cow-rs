| `PROP-CORE-018` | `cow-sdk-core` | `Amount::parse_units(value, decimals)` is the exact, integer-only token-amount constructor (the typed analogue of viem/ethers `parseUnits`) and fails closed where the raw `alloy_primitives::utils::parse_units` fails open.
